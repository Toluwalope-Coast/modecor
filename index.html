About Modecor Ventures

Modecor Ventures is a woman-owned interior decoration brand based in Ibadan, Nigeria. Our primary focus is helping customers shop luxurious interior décor pieces in our walk-in showroom/store— including premium frames, wall décor, and statement items that elevate spaces instantly.

We also provide professional interior styling and setup services for homes, offices, stores, shortlets, and hospitality spaces. We serve Ibadan and deliver projects across Nigeria, with support for select projects across Africa.

What you can expect:
• Curated luxury pieces that feel premium
• Honest recommendations that match your space
• Clean finishing and a polished final look
